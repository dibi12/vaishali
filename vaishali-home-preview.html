Est. — The House

Where ancient
textile heritage
meets global
haute couture.

Vaishali Shadangule built her house on the loom. Every collection begins not on a sketch pad but in conversation with weavers — the inheritors of India's most ancient textile traditions. The result is couture that belongs simultaneously to the ateliers of Paris and the handloom workshops of rural India.

The first Indian woman to present at Paris Haute Couture Week. The first Indian designer to open a flagship in Paris. Each milestone a quiet declaration: this is a global luxury house.

The Space

"The atelier is where the conversation between weaver and wearer begins."

Nestled in the historic Kala Ghoda district of Mumbai's Fort area, the Vaishali S flagship is more than a boutique — it is an archive of living craft. Within these walls, each handwoven garment hangs as a testament to the artisans who spent weeks, sometimes months, at the loom. Visitors are invited to touch, to ask, to understand the provenance of every thread. Personal styling consultations are available by appointment.

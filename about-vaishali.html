Home — Vaishali Shadangule

Founder & Creative Director

Vaishali
Shadangule

Vaishali Shadangule is an acclaimed Indian fashion designer celebrated for her dedication to traditional handlooms and pioneering sustainable couture. Originally from Vidisha, Madhya Pradesh, her journey began at seventeen — when she left home without support, driven by a desire for self-expression.

The Journey

She spent time in Bhopal, where she encountered new perspectives, having grown up in a small village where fashion was unfamiliar. Despite studying engineering, Vaishali developed a passion for styling and design. While holding various jobs to support herself, she would bring her sketchbook everywhere, sharing her designs whenever she could.

A pivotal moment came when she worked at a gym, offering fashion advice to members. Recognizing her talent, the wife of a bank manager provided her with a loan of INR 50,000 — enabling Vaishali to start her brand. She worked tirelessly in Mumbai, opening her first store in Juhu, and, recognizing the value of education, enrolled in fashion school in Delhi. There, she faced challenges for her unconventional style but remained undeterred, later pursuing a master's in fashion design in Milan while balancing her business and family.

"A major turning point came in 2021 — when she became the first Indian woman designer to present at Paris Haute Couture Week."

Milestones

A First, Again and Again

2021

First Indian woman designer to present on the Paris Haute Couture calendar.

2022

First Indian woman on the official presentations calendar at Milan Fashion Week.

2023

Spoke at TEDx Mumbai and received the Vogue Forces of Fashion award.

2024

First Indian designer to open a flagship on Boulevard Saint-Germain, Paris.

Sustainability

The only designer globally working exclusively with handloom.

Over the past years, Vaishali has remained the only designer in the world to work exclusively with handloom, carrying these living fabrics to an international audience. Vaishali S Couture is also a leader in environmental responsibility — adhering to zero-waste and plastic-free practices since inception.

"
I design from observation, not imagination — listening to the land, the loom, and the women who wear them.

Vaishali Shadangule

Recently

A House on the World Stage

In 2024, Vaishali S presented three new couture collections and expanded globally, becoming the first Indian designer to open a store on Boulevard Saint-Germain in Paris. The house continues to carry India's handwoven heritage to the most rarefied stages in the world — Mumbai to Milan to Paris — one thread at a time.

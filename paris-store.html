The Boutique

"Where Indian craft finds its most Parisian expression."

Nestled in the literary heart of Paris — on the same boulevard that has inspired artists, philosophers and couturiers for over a century — the Vaishali S Paris boutique is a quiet monument to slow fashion. Our Kintsugi collection debuted at Paris Haute Couture Week 2025, and the boutique now carries the full range of handwoven couture, each piece traceable to the loom and the hands that made it. Private viewing appointments are warmly encouraged.

Home — Made To Order

The Philosophy

A garment made for you, and only you.

The made-to-order service at Vaishali S is not customisation — it is creation from the first thread. We begin with a conversation about who you are and how you wish to be seen. We end with a garment that could not have existed without you.

Every piece is woven and constructed in our Mumbai atelier by artisans who have worked with the house for years. Delivery timelines range from 8 to 16 weeks depending on the complexity of the textile and construction.

How It Works

The Four Steps

01

Consultation

A private appointment — in Mumbai, Paris, or virtually — to understand your vision, occasion, proportions, and how you wish to carry the garment.

In-person or virtual Mumbai · Paris · Worldwide 45 – 90 minutes
02

Fabric Selection

We present our textile archive — handwoven silks from Murshidabad, natural fibres, and experimental weaves developed exclusively for the house over the season.

Murshidabad silk Maheshwar cotton Chanderi & natural fibres
03

Crafting

The garment is built by our atelier team — draped, cut, and sewn to your precise measurements. A minimum of two fittings are scheduled during construction.

8 – 16 weeks construction 2+ fittings included Hand-finished throughout
04

Worldwide Delivery

Your piece arrives in bespoke packaging, secured and insured, with a textile provenance card documenting the weave origin and artisan community.

Insured international shipping Textile provenance card Atelier care instructions

The Collection

Kintsugi — the Japanese philosophy of repairing what is broken with gold.

For her F/W 2025 Paris Haute Couture presentation, Vaishali Shadangule turned to the Japanese art of kintsugi as both metaphor and method. Garments are constructed to reveal their seams — lacquer-gold thread running through handwoven silk, celebrating rupture and repair as marks of beauty rather than imperfection. Each look references a fragment: a shard of porcelain, a vein of ore, a fault line in stone.

The collection comprises 18 looks, presented in January 2025 at the Palais de Chaillot, Paris.

The House — Since 2001

Our Philosophy

Vaishali S, an eponymous label, is born out of designer Vaishali Shadangule's lifelong love for classic Indian weaves. Since its inception in 2001, the label has explored authentic hand-woven textiles — from Chanderi silk to Khunn, a Karnataka native weave that Vaishali helped revive — while perennially telling culturally rich tales of the country's heritage. Each Vaishali S creation is a masterwork of wearable art that flaunts its rich history.

The Designer

Vaishali Shadangule

Founder & Creative Director

Our founder and creative director draws inspiration from her ability to observe and listen, while letting her designs speak for themselves. Born in the small town of Vidisha, Madhya Pradesh, Vaishali realized her passion at a very young age and has been experimenting with handwoven textiles and their applications for just over two decades. A creative powerhouse, the designer extends her work with textiles to art and design, infusing it with spontaneity and zest.

More About Vaishali

Our Purpose

A new approach
to fashion.

VAISHALI S Couture exists to inspire a new approach to fashion that entirely relies on artisanal crafts. Our mission is to bring back the fading skill of hand weaving to a global luxury level — and to share its immense cultural value and social potential with the world.

What We Stand For

Craft, conscience & community

No. 01

Design & Luxury

Since our inception, we have directed our focus entirely upon modernizing traditional hand-woven textile infrastructure, believing in its ability to ensure sustainability and lessen environmental impact. It is our unspoken promise to artisans and customers alike to use natural resources and create products of the highest quality. Craftsmanship and design run proudly through the veins of our brand.

No. 02

Environmental
Sustainability

Vaishali S believes in going back to the old, and to the simpler ways of living. Our founder's upbringing, coupled with her natural direction towards minimalistic production, have become a manual for us to follow. Not an inch of fabric goes to waste within our production unit; by using only materials of the finest quality, we improve garment longevity — and push forward a principle of sustainability we have carried for over twenty years.

No. 03

Social Sustainability

We consistently focus on elevating the status of weavers across the country, empowering them to make informed choices that progressively enrich their lives. Since 2001, Vaishali S has adopted over 3,000 families across India, aiming to create a society that guarantees their livelihood for years to come. We've added priorities to engage women into our supply chain, and to support our own.

No. 04

Our Weavers

In the last decade, VAISHALI S Couture has become a modern window into the world of Indian luxury craftsmanship. Since 2001, we have explored Assam, West Bengal, Rajasthan, Maharashtra, Madhya Pradesh and Karnataka, rediscovering over fifteen different weaving techniques. We wish to continue this journey — to discover all the hand-weaving traditions of India, and the world.

No. 05

Our Weaves
& Textures

We take pride in the fact that each of our fabrics is thoughtfully engineered by our creative director. Only through textiles woven by hand are we able to create such intricate motifs and patterns. We actively work on shortening our supply chain — allowing our weavers to be paid above the norm, and giving them respect, personal interaction, and recognition.

“Every collection begins not on a sketch pad, but in conversation with the weavers.”

Vaishali Shadangule
